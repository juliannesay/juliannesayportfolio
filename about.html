About Julianne

Hello! My name is Julianne Say. I am originally from Virginia Beach, VA, and currently reside in Richmond, VA. I am a GIS Specialist for the City of Richmond and am working on my Masters in Cartography and GIS concentrated in GIS Development from the Univeristy of Wisconsin- Madison.

Although I am early in my career, I believe my experiences thus far have given me a robust and diverse GIS background. I am open to both GIS volunteer and freelance work, so please review my resume below to learn more about my background.

Hailing from a beach town, I have an infinity for being near the water, collecting seashells, and kayaking. I also enjoy camping, going on walks, cooking, and going to the movies.
